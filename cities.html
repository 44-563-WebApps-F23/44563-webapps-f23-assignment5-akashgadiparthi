As I reduce the browser window size, I notice that the cities row is the first to break. This happens because we set the breakpoint for the cities row using col-md, which means that on small screens (768px and below), the letters will start stacking vertically. The letter names, specified with col-sm-2, will continue to display horizontally until the screen becomes even narrower.

DELHI

Delhi, city and national capital territory, north-central India. The city of Delhi actually consists of two components: Old Delhi, in the north, the historic city; and New Delhi, in the south, since 1947 the capital of India, built in the first part of the 20th century as the capital of British India.
